# File Scaffolds

These templates are used by the entry skill to scaffold new research and draft files.

## research.md Template

When a new entry is created, `research.md` is initialized with this structure:

```markdown
# Research: [Your Topic]

## Rough Idea

[Your initial thoughts go here]

## References & Sources

- [Links and citations]

## Observations

[Insights as you explore]
```

## draft.md Template (With Title)

When moving to draft stage and the user provides a title, `draft.md` is created with the H1 pre-filled:

```markdown
# Your Title Here

[Your entry body]
```

## draft.md Template (Deferred Title)

When moving to draft stage without a title yet, `draft.md` is created with a placeholder:

```markdown
# [Add your title here]

[Your entry body]
```

## Notes

- The `research.md` template provides three sections to guide thinking: initial idea, sources, and observations
- The `draft.md` templates preserve the H1 title throughout the workflow
- Users can add more sections to research or draft at any point—these templates just provide a starting scaffold
